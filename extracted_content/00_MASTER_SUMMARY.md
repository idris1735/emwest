## WHAT IS EMWA?
EMWA (Equipment & Manufacturing West Africa) is Nigeria's premier international 
trade exhibition for Industrial Products, Manufacturing Equipment & Machinery. 
It's a B2B event organized by Zenith Exhibitions, held at the Landmark Centre 
in Lagos, Nigeria.

## KEY DETAILS
- **Event Name:** EMWA - Equipment & Manufacturing West Africa
- **Type:** International Trade Exhibition & Conference
- **Organizer:** Zenith Exhibitions (20+ years experience)
- **Venue:** Landmark Centre, Lagos, Nigeria
- **Address:** Plot 2 & 3, Water Corporation Drive, Victoria Island, Annex, Lagos
- **Next Event:** 25-27 May 2027
- **Previous Event:** 30 May - 01 June 2023 (3rd Annual)
- **Attendees:** 3,000+ industry professionals / 3,700+ professional buyers
- **Website:** https://emwestafrica.com
